About

I am a second-year PhD student in Computing at Newcastle University in the United Kingdom. Within the university, I am part of the Cloud Computing for Big Data CDT and the Fluid Dynamics Lab.

My research focuses on computer emulators and optimisation of physical experiments and computer simulations.
